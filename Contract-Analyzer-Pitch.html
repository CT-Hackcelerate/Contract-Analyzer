Hours

$$$

Why you can trust it

Anti-hallucination by design

Evidence for every finding

Deterministic re-computation

Counts, contract status, monetary exposure, and page numbers are computed in code from the evidence.

EM ↔ pricing reconciliation

Safety & compliance

Built for sensitive, healthcare-adjacent data

No real PHI/PII

Synthetic samples only; uploads deleted immediately after processing; logs & audit trail PII-masked (email, phone, PAN, Aadhaar, SSN, MRN, keys).

PHI screening (above & beyond)

Every upload is scanned for MRN, DOB, SSN, ICD codes, claim/beneficiary IDs, NPI — any hit is flagged and audited.

Prompt-injection defense

The contract is treated as untrusted data inside markers; embedded "instructions" are ignored and flagged.

Human-in-the-loop

A decision-support aid, not legal advice — it recommends, never auto-executes; unseen referenced docs are flagged for review.
